Who we are

What makes us different

We're not a studio or an agency. We're a startup team that builds, ships, and iterates on our own products — with full ownership from day one.

Speed. Every product starts as a hypothesis. We validate in days, not months — prototyping fast, testing with real users, and killing what doesn't work before it wastes resources.

Ownership. There's no "client handoff" here. Every team member is invested in the outcome. We build what we believe in, and we stand behind every release.

Engineering quality. Clean architecture, tested code, scalable infrastructure. We build things that last — not throwaway demos.
